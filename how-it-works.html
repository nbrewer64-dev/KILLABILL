Getting Started

You're live in
three steps.

01
Open or connect your brokerage account

You need an options-enabled brokerage account at one of our supported brokers. If you don't have one, Alpaca is free to open and takes about 5 minutes. You'll need at least Options Level 2 for spread strategies — your broker guides you through that on signup.

Once your account is set up, generate an API key with trade-only permissions. Trade-only means Kill A Bill can place and manage positions, but cannot withdraw funds, transfer money, or change any account settings. It is physically not possible.

⚡ Alpaca users are typically live within 10 minutes
02
Connect through Telegram

Search @KillABill_bot on Telegram and send /start. The bot walks you through full onboarding — broker selection, API credentials, automatic connection verification. No emails, no forms, no waiting.

Once connected, the system reads your account size via API and calibrates the right strategy parameters automatically. You don't select a plan or configure anything. It just knows.

📱 All controls live in Telegram — no separate app needed
03
The system takes it from there

At the next market open (9:30 AM ET), Kill A Bill begins scanning for opportunities. The spread bot looks for qualifying setups each week. The LEAPS growth engine starts building its position over the first few weeks.

Every Friday at 4:05 PM ET, you get a full plain-English summary of what happened in your account. When your growth position hits its profit target, you get a notification with a 24-hour window to take profits or let them auto-reinvest.

FAQ

Common questions,
straight answers.

Can you access or withdraw my money?
No. API credentials grant trade-execution permissions only. It is technically not possible for Kill A Bill to initiate withdrawals, transfers, or any changes to your account settings. Your brokerage relationship is entirely yours.
Do I need to know anything about options?
No. The system handles all execution, monitoring, and risk management. You receive plain-language notifications for every significant event. The weekly summaries are written for regular people, not traders.
What happens if the market crashes?
A VIX circuit breaker automatically halts new spread entries when market fear spikes. Existing positions are managed to their natural exits. The delta stop exits LEAPS positions that deteriorate past threshold. Both the 2020 COVID crash and the 2022 bear market are fully represented in the backtest data — the strategy returned +42.4% in 2022.
How do I actually access my profits?
Realized profits from spreads accumulate in your brokerage account automatically. When a LEAPS position hits its target, you receive a Telegram notification. Send /take within 24 hours to keep the profit in your pool for withdrawal through your broker — otherwise it auto-reinvests into the next LEAPS cycle.
What if I want to stop?
Send /pause to halt new trade entry immediately. Existing positions continue to be managed to their natural conclusion. To fully disconnect, revoke API access through your broker's settings. No positions are orphaned. No friction.
Is this legal? Do you need to be licensed?
Kill A Bill charges a flat subscription fee for software access — not a percentage of assets or profits. This is the same model as any trading platform or tool.
