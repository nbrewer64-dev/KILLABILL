You need an options-enabled brokerage account at a supported broker. Options Level 2 or higher is required. If you already have one at a supported broker, you're good — skip ahead to Step 3. If Schwab or Webull require advance approval, start that application now before moving on.

SECRET: your_app_secret

Step 4 of 4

You're live. Here's
what happens next.

Once connected, the system begins at the next market open. Here's exactly what to expect and when.

📲
Day 1
You're connected and confirmed

The bot sends a confirmation with your selected tier and strategy details. Your account is now being monitored. The spread bot begins scanning for the first qualifying setup at 9:30 AM ET on the next trading day.

💸
Days 1–7
First spread position entered

The spread bot finds a qualifying setup and places the first bull put spread. You'll see the position appear in your broker account in real time. The LEAPS queue begins accumulating simultaneously.

📈
Weeks 2–8
LEAPS growth position opens

Once the LEAPS queue reaches its threshold, the growth engine opens its first position. Both the spread income bot and LEAPS growth engine are now running in parallel. Spread income starts accumulating in your pool.

📅
Every Friday
Weekly summary at 4:05 PM ET

Every Friday afternoon you receive a full plain-English account summary — positions, income collected that week, running P&L, and anything noteworthy. No jargon. Just your numbers.

🔔
Months 12–18
First LEAPS harvest notification

When your LEAPS position reaches 2.25x its effective cost basis, you receive a Telegram notification. You have 24 hours to send /take and direct those profits to your pool for withdrawal — or do nothing and they automatically reinvest into the next cycle.
